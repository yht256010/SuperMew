Hybrid Precision.

Semantic meaning isn't enough. We built a high-concurrency, lock-protected BM25 Sparse Vector engine from scratch.

Milvus simultaneously executes HNSW (Dense) and Inverted Index (Sparse) searches, fusing them flawlessly with RRF (Reciprocal Rank Fusion).

Unbroken Context.

Parent-Child Chunking prevents context fragmentation.

Documents are sliced into 3 tiers. Only the micro-sentences (L3) enter Milvus. Once hit, SuperMew seamlessly resurrects the macro-paragraphs (L1/L2) to feed the LLM the complete picture.
